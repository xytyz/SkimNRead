The Story Behind Skimmy.

As the COVID-19 Virus took over our livelihood,all across the globe research and studies had to focus on getting the best results in order to combat this situation. Amidst this chaos,large amounts of resources were being spent and of course , time was of the essence. This valuable time was getting spent of reading through multi-paged articles only to find it is not related to the field of research needed!


To combat this situation ,Skimmy was created by our team members.It allows researchers and students to 'Skim' long abstratcs into shorter targeted categories aid the process.
